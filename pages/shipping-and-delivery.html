NORMAL SHIPPING

Our Processing time for each shipemt is 2-3working days, once it is shipped it takes 2to 9 working days max to reach you from shipping. Please calculate National/Local Holidays and Weekends as holidays.

Please be aware that the contract we have with the courier company entitles delivery to be tried to the receiver location 2 to 3 times in case of any delivery issues. This has been enabled to benefit our customers to receive the parcel at any cost. But beyond the specified limit of trying to deliver the parcel at the mentioned address by the customer, the parcel shall be retained at the courier company for pickup by the customer themselves. Again, retainment of the parcel at the courier office after multiple unsuccessful deliveries depends on the location and the courier service. The customer is requested to provide an address where someone would be available to collect the parcel during courier service normal working hours to avoid delivery failures. On account of failure, they may reach out to us on email on the below mentioned id for the address of the courier office to collect the same.
